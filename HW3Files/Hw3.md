Problem 1

The two degree of freedom brussellator is given by:

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
\dot{x_1} \\
\dot{x_2}
\end{bmatrix} = 
\begin{bmatrix}
1+x_{1}^2x_{2}-3x_1-x_1 \\
-x_{1}^2x_{2}+3x_1
\end{bmatrix}
$$ 

Consider an observable of this system:

$$u(\vec{x})=x_1+ x_2$$

The Louville PDE for the system is given by:

$$u_t=(1+z_{1}^2z_2 - 3z_1 - z_1)u_{z_1} + (3z_1-z_{1}^2z_2)u_{z_2}$$
